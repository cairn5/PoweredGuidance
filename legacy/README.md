# legacy

The original standalone `navbox` prototype — a self-contained OpenGL app with its own
UPFG port, gravity-turn ascent, mission/target config, and a 3D visualizer.

Kept for reference only. It is **not** built, referenced, or shipped by the mod; its
guidance was superseded by `ksamod/Upfg/` (ascent) and the `gfold/` solver (descent),
and its visualization by the in-game world overlay. `navbox.sln` builds it in isolation
if needed.

The `*.md` files here are design notes from that era.
